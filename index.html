About Us

E and B Farm is an rural family-owned farm located at the small town of San Tedoro, Oriental Mindoro. Our business is aimed to produce world-class oyster mushrooms products and develop and innovate new ways of producing healthy and delicous snack with various organic crops that are grown in-house.
